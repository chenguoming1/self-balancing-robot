HOW TO USE
1. Pair your JDY-23 with this PC over Bluetooth — it shows as a COM port
2. Click Connect Serial and pick that COM port
3. Baud rate is set to 9600 automatically
4. The 3D robot model rotates live with the real pitch angle from MPU6050
5. Use Pause to freeze the charts without stopping data
6. Toggle individual series with the buttons above the charts
